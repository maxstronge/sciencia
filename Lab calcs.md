$$
V_{\text{out}}=V_{\text{in}}\left( \frac{R_{33}}{R_{11}+R_{33}} \right) \
$$

$$
V_{in}=V_{out}\left( \frac{R_{11}+R_{33}}{R_{33}} \right) = 2.1529
$$

$$
\text{Gain}=\frac{V_{out}}{V_{in}}=\frac{1.6}{2.1529}=0.7431
